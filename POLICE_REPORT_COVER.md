## Talking points (say these at the counter)

- "This is ongoing, targeted harassment — silent calls from a constantly
  changing set of numbers. I believe they're spoofed."
- "I've documented every call with timestamps. Here's a one-page summary; I have
  the full log."
- "I've already filed an FCC spoofing complaint and opened an AT&T harassment
  case — here are the numbers. I want a police report so AT&T can release the
  records to you."
- "Can you give me the report number today? I need to give it back to AT&T."

## What to leave with
- A copy of your **police report number**. Then give that number to AT&T's
  harassment team so the carrier, FCC, and police files all cross-reference.

## Safety note
If any call has included **threats, or you feel you're in danger**, say that
first and clearly — it changes how urgently police treat the report, and it may
justify faster action on the records.
